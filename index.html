Features

Everything your skin needs,
in one place.

From building your first routine to tracking long-term skin progress, Skindare covers every step with precision and clarity.

Smart Routine Builder

Design your AM and PM routines step by step. Skindare intelligently orders your products and flags application conflicts before you even start.

Ingredient Safety Alerts

Real-time warnings when you combine Vitamin C + Niacinamide, Retinol + AHA/BHA, or other reactive actives. Protect your skin barrier automatically.

Personalized Skin Profile

Set your skin type, concerns, and sensitivities once. Skindare tailors every recommendation and alert to your unique profile from day one.

Product Management

Log all your skincare products with photos and key ingredients. Search your collection, mark actives, and keep everything organized and accessible.

History Tracking

Review your daily logs, streak data, and progress over time. See patterns, identify what works, and make informed adjustments to your routine.

Clean and Simple UI

Built with Flutter for a native experience on every device. Every interaction is intentional — no bloat, no clutter, just skincare made effortless.

Process

Up and running in minutes.

Getting started with Skindare is designed to be as frictionless as possible, so you can focus on your skin, not the setup.

1

Create your account

Sign up in seconds using email or your existing accounts. Your data is secured with Firebase Authentication and never shared with third parties.

2

Set your skin profile

Answer a short set of questions about your skin type, primary concerns, known sensitivities, and current skincare goals. This powers every recommendation.

3

Add your products

Photograph and log each product in your collection. Skindare reads key active ingredients and builds a reference library unique to your cabinet.

4

Build your routine

Drag products into your AM and PM sequences. Ingredient conflicts are surfaced instantly, and the smart order engine optimizes for maximum effectiveness.

5

Track and improve

Log each session, maintain your streak, and review your history weekly. Watch your skin improve and refine your routine as you learn what works.
